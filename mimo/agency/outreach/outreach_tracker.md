# Outreach Tracker - Copy to Google Sheets or Excel

## Columns:
A: Clinic Name
B: Website
C: Owner/Manager Name
D: LinkedIn URL
E: City
F: Connection Sent (date)
G: Accepted (Y/N)
H: Message Sent (date)
I: Replied (Y/N)
J: Reply Text
K: Demo Booked (date)
L: Status
