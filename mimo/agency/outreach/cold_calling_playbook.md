# Cold Calling Playbook: Dental Clinics

## Before You Call

### Research (2 min per clinic)

1. **Google the clinic**
   - Name, address, phone
   - Hours of operation
   - Number of reviews

2. **Check reviews**
   - Any complaints about response time?
   - Note specific quotes: "nobody answered", "never called back"

3. **Check website**
   - Online booking? (Probably no)
   - Contact form? (Probably no)
   - Hours listed? (Note closing time)

4. **Find decision maker**
   - Owner name (LinkedIn, website "About" page)
   - Office manager name (sometimes listed)
   - If no name → ask for "person who handles operations"

### Your Setup

- **Time to call:** Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-12pm or 2pm-4pm
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, lunch hours
- **Environment:** Quiet, no distractions
- **Have ready:** Clinic info, your script, pen/paper

---

## The Call Script

### Opening (10 seconds)

```
"Hi, this is {your name} from MiMo AI. Is this {name / the owner / 
the person who handles operations}?"
```

**If yes:** Continue
**If no:** "Could you connect me to them? It's about helping with patient bookings."
**If gatekeeper:** "I'm calling about a way to capture missed patient calls. 
Who handles that for you?"

---

### Permission + Hook (15 seconds)

```
"I'll be brief. I noticed {clinic} doesn't have online booking on your website, 
and I wanted to show you something that might help capture more patients.

Do you have 60 seconds?"
```

**Why this works:**
- You noticed something specific (shows you did homework)
- It's about THEM (capturing patients)
- You ask for permission (respectful)
- You ask for 60 seconds (low commitment)

---

### Problem Statement (30 seconds)

```
"Here's the thing: when someone calls {clinic} after 5pm or on weekends, 
what happens?

Most clinics either go to voicemail or miss the call entirely. 
And 80% of people who hit voicemail don't leave a message — they just call 
the next clinic on Google.

I checked: {clinic} closes at {time} on weekdays and is closed Sundays. 
That's a lot of missed opportunities."
```

**Pause. Let them respond.**

---

### Solution (30 seconds)

```
"We built an AI system that answers those calls in 60 seconds. 
Books the appointment. Sends confirmation. Follows up.

Your front desk arrives to booked appointments instead of voicemails.

Would you like to see how it works?"
```

---

### Handle Responses

#### "Tell me more"
```
"Sure. Basically, it's like having a receptionist that never sleeps.

When someone calls after hours, our AI answers, qualifies them, 
and books the appointment right into your system.

Clinics using this capture 15-20 extra appointments per month.

Want me to show you a 2-minute demo?"
```

#### "How much does it cost?"
```
"It's $1,500/month. But let me ask you this:

How many patients do you think you miss per month from after-hours calls?"

[Let them answer]

"If it's even 5 patients, and each patient is worth $500-1,000, 
that's $2,500-5,000 in lost revenue. The system pays for itself."

Want to see a quick demo?"
```

#### "We already have a system"
```
"That's great. Most clinics we talk to have a voicemail system. 

The difference is: our AI actually books the appointment. 
It doesn't just take a message — it gets them on the schedule.

Is your current system booking appointments after hours, or just 
taking messages?"
```

#### "I'm not interested"
```
"No problem. Can I ask — is it because you already have a solution, 
or because you don't see the need?

I ask because I noticed {specific thing from their website/reviews} 
and thought it might be relevant."
```

#### "Send me an email"
```
"Absolutely. What's your email?

But real quick — what questions do you have right now that would 
help you decide? I want to make sure I send you the right info."
```

**Then book the follow-up call:**
```
"I'll send that over today. Should I follow up tomorrow or 
Thursday to see what you think?"
```

---

### Close

```
"Here's what I suggest:

Let me show you a 2-minute demo on a quick call. I'll show you exactly 
how this works for {clinic}.

Do you have time tomorrow morning, or is afternoon better?"
```

**Never end without a next step.**

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "What if I showed you it pays for itself with 2 extra patients?" |
| "We're fine" | "Most clinics we work with thought the same. Then they saw the missed calls data." |
| "Not now" | "Totally understand. When would be a better time to revisit?" |
| "Send info" | "I will. What questions do you have right now?" |
| "We have staff" | "This isn't replacing staff — it's catching what falls through after hours." |
| "Already have system" | "Is it booking appointments, or just taking messages?" |
| "Call back later" | "Sure. When specifically? I'll put it in my calendar." |
| "I'm busy" | "I'll be brief. 30 seconds — is after-hours patient capture an issue for you?" |

---

## Tone and Delivery

### Do:
- Speak slowly and clearly
- Sound confident, not pushy
- Use their name
- Pause after questions
- Listen more than you talk
- Take notes

### Don't:
- Rush through script
- Sound like a telemarketer
- Argue or push too hard
- Talk about "AI" too much (they don't care)
- End without next step

---

## After the Call

### If interested:
1. Send follow-up email within 1 hour
2. Include: demo link, ROI breakdown, next steps
3. Book the demo call

### If not interested:
1. Thank them
2. Ask: "When should I follow up?"
3. Log in tracker

### If no answer:
1. Leave voicemail (30 seconds max):
```
"Hi, this is {name} from MiMo AI. I help dental clinics capture 
missed after-hours calls. Quick question for you — call me back 
at {number}. Thanks."
```
2. Follow up in 3 days
3. Try again at different time

---

## Voicemail Script (30 seconds)

```
"Hi, this is {name} from MiMo AI.

I help dental clinics capture patient calls that come in after hours.

Quick question for you — call me back at {your number}.

That's {repeat number}. Thanks."
```

**Why it works:**
- Short (they won't listen to long messages)
- Clear value (capture patient calls)
- Specific ask (call back)
- Number repeated

## Daily Call Routine

### Morning (10am-12pm)
1. Review 10 clinics from list
2. Research each (2 min)
3. Make 8-10 calls
4. Log results

### Afternoon (2pm-4pm)
1. Follow up on morning calls
2. Make 5-8 more calls
3. Send emails to interested leads

### End of Day
1. Update tracker
2. Prepare next day's list
3. Send any pending follow-ups
