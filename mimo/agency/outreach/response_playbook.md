# Response Handling Playbook

## Response Categories

### 1. Positive (Interested)
- "Sounds interesting"
- "Tell me more"
- "How does it work?"
- "What's the price?"

### 2. Neutral (Question)
- "What do you mean?"
- "How is this different?"
- "Do you have examples?"

### 3. Negative (Not interested)
- "Not interested"
- "We're good"
- "Too expensive"
- "Not now"

### 4. No Response (after 3 days)

---

## Scripts by Response Type

### Positive Response → Book the Call

```
"Great! When works best for you?

I can do:
- Quick 15-min demo call (I'll show you exactly how this works for clinics)
- Video walkthrough (I'll send a link)

What's your preference?"
```

**If they give a time:**
```
"Perfect. I'll send you a calendar invite for {day} at {time}.

Before the call, I'll put together a quick analysis of {clinic} so I can show you specifically how this would work for you.

What's your email for the invite?"
```

---

### Neutral Response → Provide Value

**"What do you mean?"**
```
"Good question. Let me be specific:

Right now, when someone calls {clinic} after 6pm or on weekends, what happens?

Most clinics either:
1. Go to voicemail (and lose the patient)
2. Have someone check messages next day (too late)

We fix this with AI that responds in 60 seconds, 24/7. Books the appointment automatically.

Want to see a quick demo?"
```

**"How is this different from a regular answering service?"**
```
"Great question. Three key differences:

1. Speed: AI responds in 60 seconds vs. 5-10 minutes for human services
2. Cost: $1,500/month vs. $3,000-5,000 for 24/7 human staff
3. Intelligence: AI qualifies leads, books appointments, and follows up automatically

Plus, no missed calls during busy periods when your receptionist is on the phone."
```

**"Do you have examples?"**
```
"Yes. We just helped a dental clinic in {city} increase their appointment bookings by 35% in the first month.

The key: they were missing 40% of after-hours calls. Our AI captured all of those.

Want to see how this would work for {clinic}?"
```

---

### Negative Response → Reframe or Respect

**"Not interested"**
```
"No problem. Quick question though:

Is it because you already have a solution in place, or because you don't see the need?

Just curious — I want to make sure I'm not wasting your time."
```

**"We're good"**
```
"Totally understand. Most clinics we work with were also 'good' before they realized how much revenue they were leaving on the table.

Quick stat: the average clinic loses $5,000-10,000/month from missed after-hours calls.

If that resonates, I'm happy to show you the numbers for {clinic}. If not, no worries at all."
```

**"Too expensive"**
```
"I hear you. Let me ask this:

If I could show you that this system brings in just 2 extra patients per month, would it be worth it?

Average patient value for a dental clinic is $500-1,000. So 2 patients = $1,000-2,000.

The system costs $1,500/month. So if it brings in 2 patients, you're breaking even or making money.

Sound fair?"
```

**"Not now"**
```
"Totally understand. When would be a better time?

I can set a reminder to follow up in a month, or whenever works for you."
```

Takes 10 minutes to see. Worth it?"
```

**Day 7:**
```

- Leads that come in at 9pm go cold
- Patients book with competitors
- You lose $5,000+ per month

[Include a specific observation about their business]

**Day 14 (Last attempt):**
```

I've helped 3 dental clinics in {area} increase bookings by 30%+ using AI follow-up.

If you ever want to explore this, I'm here. Otherwise, no more follow-ups from me.

Wishing {clinic} continued success!"
```

## Response Tracking

| Response Type | Action | Follow-up |
|---------------|--------|-----------|
| Positive | Book call | Send calendar invite |
| Neutral | Provide value | Follow up in 3 days |
| Negative | Reframe once | Follow up in 7 days |
| No response | Follow up | Day 3, 7, 14 |

---

## Key Principles

1. **Respond within 5 minutes** (if possible)
2. **Always provide value** — don't just say "thanks"
3. **One CTA per message** — don't overwhelm
4. **Use their name and company** — personalization matters
5. **Track everything** — know where each lead stands
