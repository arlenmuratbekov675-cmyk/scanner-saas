# Outreach Templates

## LinkedIn Messages

### Template 1: Cold Outreach (Short)
```
Hi {name},

I was looking at {company} and noticed you might be missing automated lead follow-up.

We've helped similar {niche} businesses increase conversions by 40% without extra ad spend.

Would you be open to a quick 15-min demo?
```

### Template 2: Cold Outreach (With Value)
```
Hi {name},

I analyzed {company} and found 3 areas where AI could bring you more customers:

1. Automated lead response (capture leads 24/7)
2. Smart follow-up (never miss a prospect)
3. Lead scoring (focus on hot leads only)

I built a demo specifically for {niche} businesses. Want to see it?
```

### Template 3: After They View Your Profile
```
Hi {name},

Thanks for checking out my profile. I help {niche} businesses automate their lead generation.

Quick question: how do you currently handle leads that come in after hours?
```

### Template 4: Mutual Connection
```
Hi {name},

{mutual_connection} suggested I reach out. I help businesses like {company} automate their customer acquisition.

Would love to share how we helped {similar_company} increase leads by 3x in 30 days.
```

[Your name]

## Response Handling

### If interested:
```
Great! When works best for you? I can do:
- Quick 15-min demo call
- Video walkthrough (I'll send a link)
- In-person meeting (if local)

What's your preference?
```

### If price objection:
```
Understood. Our entry package starts at $500 — that's less than what you'd spend on one bad ad campaign.

The ROI is simple: if we bring you just 2 extra customers per month, it pays for itself.

Want to see the numbers for your specific business?
```

### If not now:
```
No problem. When would be a better time to revisit this? I can set a reminder to follow up in a month.
```

### If competitor mentioned:
```
That's great you're exploring options. What we do differently is [unique value prop].

I can show you a demo so you can compare. Would that help?
```

---

## Tips

1. Personalize EVERY message (mention company name, niche, specific detail)
2. Keep it short (under 100 words for LinkedIn)
3. One CTA per message
4. Follow up 2-3 times (most responses come on follow-up)
5. Track what works and iterate
