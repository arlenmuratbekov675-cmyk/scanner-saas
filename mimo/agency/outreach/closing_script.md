# Closing Script: 15-Minute Demo Call

## Pre-Call Prep (5 min before)

1. Research their clinic:
   - Website: check for booking system
   - Google reviews: check response time complaints
   - Social media: see their activity
   - Hours: note if they have after-hours gaps

2. Know their pain:
   - "They probably miss calls after 6pm"
   - "No online booking visible"
   - "Reviews mention slow response"

---

## Call Structure (15 min max)

### Opening (2 min)

```
"Hi {name}, thanks for taking the time. 

Before I show you anything, I want to understand your situation. 

How are you currently handling patient inquiries that come in after hours or on weekends?"
```

**LISTEN.** Don't talk. Let them explain.

---

### Problem Discovery (3 min)

Ask these questions:

```
1. "How many calls do you think you miss per week?"

2. "What happens when someone fills out a form on your website at 9pm?"

3. "Have you ever checked how many leads go cold because of slow response?"

4. "What's your current booking rate from first inquiry?"
```

**Write down their answers.** You'll use these later.

---

### Agitate the Problem (2 min)

Based on their answers, say:

```
"So if I understand correctly:

- You're missing about {X} calls per week
- After-hours inquiries don't get responded to until next day
- That's probably costing you {calculation} per month in lost patients

Is that right?"
```

**Let them confirm.** This makes the pain real.

---

### Present Solution (3 min)

```
"Here's what we built for clinics like yours:

[SHOW DEMO - 2 min]

The key thing: when someone calls or fills out a form at 9pm, our AI responds in 60 seconds. Books the appointment. Sends confirmation.

You wake up to booked appointments instead of missed opportunities."
```

**Show THEIR clinic in the demo if possible.**

---

### Price & Close (5 min)

```
"So here's how this works:

The system costs $1,500 per month.

But let's look at the math:

- You said you miss {X} calls per week
- Average patient value is ${Y}
- That's ${X * Y * 4} per month in lost revenue

If we recover even 20% of that, you're making back 3x the investment.

Does that make sense?"
```

**Silence.** Let them respond.

---

### Handle Objections

#### "It's too expensive"
```
"I understand. Let me ask you this:

If I could show you that this system brings in just 2 extra patients per month, would it be worth it?

Because that's what most of our clinics see."
```

#### "We already have a receptionist"
```
"Great. This isn't a replacement — it's a backup for when your receptionist is busy, after hours, or on breaks.

Think of it as a safety net that catches everything that falls through."
```

#### "I need to think about it"
```
"Totally understand. What specifically do you want to think about?

Is it the price, the technology, or something else?"

[Find the real objection]
```

#### "Can you send me more info?"
```
"Absolutely. I'll send you a one-pager with the ROI breakdown.

But real quick — what questions do you have right now that would help you decide?"
```

---

### Final Close

```
"So here's what I suggest:

Let's start with a 30-day trial. If it doesn't bring in at least 5 extra booked appointments, you don't pay.

Sound fair?"
```

**Or:**

```
"Most clinics start with our Growth package at $1,500/month.

I can get you set up today, and we'll have it running by end of week.

Should I send you the agreement?"
```

---

## Post-Call

1. Send follow-up email within 1 hour
2. Include: ROI calculation, next steps, agreement
3. Schedule follow-up call if needed

---

## Key Rules

1. **Listen more than you talk** (70/30 rule)
2. **Never argue** — agree and reframe
3. **Use their numbers** — "you said {X}"
4. **Create urgency** — "each week without this costs you {Y}"
5. **Make it easy** — one clear next step
