# LinkedIn-Only Outreach Plan

## The Rule

**Only LinkedIn. No email. No calls. For now.**

Why: One channel = focused learning. You'll see what works fast.

---

## Step 1: Find 30 Clinics (30 min)

### Google Search
```
dental clinic {your city}
```

### For each clinic:
1. Open website
2. Check: online booking? No = continue
3. Google the clinic name + owner name
4. Find on LinkedIn

### Add to list:
```
Clinic name | Website | Owner/Manager name | LinkedIn URL
```

**Goal: 30 clinics with LinkedIn profiles**

---

## Step 2: Connect (No Message Yet)

### Send connection request:

```
Hi {name},

quick question — do you ever lose patients from missed calls or after-hours inquiries at {clinic}?
```

**That's it.** No pitch. No sell. Just connect.

### Why?
- They see your profile
- They accept (or don't)
- You're now "connected"
- Future messages go to primary inbox

**Send 30 connection requests today.**

---

## Step 3: Wait 2-3 Days

Let them see your profile. Let them accept.

Don't message yet.

---

## Step 4: Send Message (After They Accept)

### Message 1 (Day 3-4 after connection):

```
Thanks for connecting, {name}.

Most dental clinics I looked at lose 10–30% of potential patients because calls come in after hours or don't get followed up properly.

Is that something you notice at {clinic}?
```

**Stop.** Don't say more. Wait for reply.

---

## Step 5: Handle Responses

### They reply "Voicemail" or "We miss calls":

```
"That's exactly what we fix. 

We built an AI that answers in 60 seconds and books the appointment.

Want to see a 2-min demo?"
```

### They reply "We have a system":

```
"Good. Quick question — does it book appointments, 
or just take messages?"
```

### They don't reply:

**Wait 3 days. Then:**

```
Hey {name}, just following up — is after-hours patient 
capture an issue for {clinic}?
```

### Still no reply:

**Wait 5 days. Then:**

```
Hi {name}, last follow-up from me.

If capturing missed calls isn't a priority right now, 
no worries. Just let me know.

Otherwise, happy to show you how we help clinics like {clinic}.
```

**Then stop.** Move on.
