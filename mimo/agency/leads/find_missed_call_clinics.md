# How to Find Clinics With Missed Call Problem

## Signal #1: Google Reviews (Most Reliable)

### What to look for:

**Negative reviews about response time:**
- "Called multiple times, no answer"
- "Left a message, never got back to me"
- "Tried to book appointment, couldn't reach anyone"
- "Voicemail full"
- "Waited days for a callback"

**Search queries:**
```
"{clinic name}" reviews
"{clinic name}" google reviews
```

### How to find:
1. Search "{city} dental clinic" on Google
2. Click on each clinic
3. Read 1-star and 2-star reviews
4. Look for response time complaints

### Example:
```
Clinic: Smile Dental NYC
Review: "I called 3 times on Tuesday afternoon, nobody answered. 
Left a voicemail. It's been 5 days, still no callback."
→ THIS IS YOUR LEAD
```

## Signal #2: Website Analysis

### Red flags (signs of no automation):

| What to Check | Problem | Score |
|---------------|---------|-------|
| No online booking | Manual calls only | +20 |
| No contact form | Hard to reach | +15 |
| No live chat | No instant response | +15 |
| Limited hours listed | After-hours gaps | +10 |
| No email listed | Can't reach via email | +10 |
| Outdated website | Low tech adoption | +10 |
| No social media links | Low engagement | +5 |

**Score 50+ = High probability of missed calls**

### How to check:
1. Visit their website
2. Look for "Book Now" button
3. Check if there's a contact form
4. Look for live chat widget
5. Check business hours

### Example:
```
Clinic: Perfect Teeth Chicago
Website: perfectteethchicago.com
- No online booking ✓
- No contact form ✓
- No live chat ✓
- Hours: M-F 9am-5pm ✓
- No weekend hours ✓
Score: 70/100 → STRONG LEAD
```

## Signal #3: Google Maps Business Hours

### What to look for:
- Closes early (4-5pm)
- No weekend hours
- Closed Sundays
- Limited Saturday hours

### Search:
```
Google Maps → "{city} dental" → Check hours
```

### Why this matters:
```
If they close at 5pm:
- Any call after 5pm = missed
- Weekend calls = missed
- That's 60+ hours/week of missed opportunities
```

---

## Signal #4: Call Them (Direct Test)

### The best test: call them yourself

**When to call:**
- After hours (6pm-9am)
- Weekend
- During lunch (12pm-1pm)

**What happens:**
- Voicemail → they miss calls
- No answer → they miss calls
- Busy signal → they're overwhelmed
- Receptionist answers → good, but test after hours

### Script:
```
Call at 7pm on Tuesday
If voicemail: "LEAD - misses after-hours calls"
If no answer: "LEAD - may be closed or understaffed"
If someone answers: "Not a lead (or test again on weekend)"
```

## Practical Workflow

### Step 1: Build Initial List (30 min)

```
1. Google: "dental clinic {city}"
2. Open 30 clinic websites in tabs
3. Quick check: online booking? Contact form? Live chat?
4. Mark: YES (has automation) or NO (doesn't have)
5. Keep the NOs
```

### Step 2: Verify with Reviews (30 min)

```
1. For each NO clinic, check Google reviews
2. Search for: "called", "answer", "response", "voicemail"
3. Count negative reviews about response time
4. Mark: HIGH (multiple complaints) or LOW (no complaints)
5. Keep the HIGHs
```

### Step 3: Test with Call (15 min)

```
1. Pick top 10 clinics
2. Call each after hours
3. Note: voicemail, no answer, busy
4. Mark: CONFIRMED (misses calls)
```

### Step 4: Final List

You now have clinics that:
- ✅ No online booking
- ✅ Complaints about response time
- ✅ Miss after-hours calls
- ✅ HIGH probability of missed revenue
