# 7-Day Launch Checklist

## Day 1-2: Build Lead List

Target: 50 businesses

How to find them:
- Google: "{niche} in {city}"
- LinkedIn: Search by industry + location
- Yelp, Google Maps, directories
- Instagram hashtags

For each lead, record:
- Company name
- Website
- Contact name (owner/manager)
- Email or LinkedIn profile
- Niche

---

## Day 3: Prepare Materials

- [ ] Landing page live (even simple one)
- [ ] Demo ready to show (sales assistant or lead gen)
- [ ] Outreach templates customized
- [ ] Tracking spreadsheet ready

---

## Day 4-5: Start Outreach

Target: 30-50 messages per day

Channels:
- LinkedIn (primary)
- Email (if available)
- Telegram (RU market)

Process:
1. Personalize message (mention their company)
2. Send
3. Log in spreadsheet
4. Follow up in 2-3 days if no response

---

## Day 6-7: Follow Up & Close

- Follow up with everyone who hasn't responded
- Schedule demos for interested leads
- Prep for demos:
  - Research their business
  - Customize demo to their niche
  - Prepare pricing

---

## Success Metrics (End of Week 1)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Leads found | 50 |
| Messages sent | 100+ |
| Responses | 10+ |
| Demos booked | 3+ |
| First client | 1+ |

---

## Demo Script (15 min)

1. "Let me show you how AI handles your leads" (2 min)
2. Show demo (5 min)
3. "Here's what this would look like for {company}" (3 min)
4. "The investment is ${price}. How does that compare to what you spend on customer acquisition now?" (3 min)
5. Close or next steps (2 min)

---

## What NOT to Do

- Don't spend more time on code
- Don't add new features
- Don't wait until it's "perfect"
- Don't skip the outreach

## What TO Do

- Send 30+ messages per day
- Talk to real humans
- Get feedback
- Iterate based on responses
