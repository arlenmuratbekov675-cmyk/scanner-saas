## #1 PICK: Dental Clinics

### Why?
- **Pain**: They miss calls, lose $500-2000 per missed patient
- **Budget**: $3-8k/month marketing spend already
- **Decision maker**: Office manager or owner (easy to reach)
- **No competition**: Most agencies don't target dental AI
- **Quick close**: They understand ROI immediately

### How to find them:
- Google: "dental clinic [city]"
- Yelp: "dentists near me"
- Google Maps: "dental office"
- Instagram: #dentist #[city]dentist

### Outreach angle:
```
Hi {name},

I noticed {clinic} might be missing patient calls after hours.

We built an AI receptionist that books appointments 24/7. 
Our dental clients capture 15-20 extra appointments per month.

Want to see how it works?
```

### Pricing:
- Starter: $500/month (AI receptionist)
- Growth: $1,500/month (receptionist + follow-up)
- Premium: $3,000/month (full automation)

## #2 PICK: Real Estate Agents

### Why?
- **Pain**: Slow lead response = lost commissions
- **Budget**: High (they earn $5-10k per deal)
- **Decision maker**: Individual agent or team lead
- **Competition**: Low for AI specifically
- **Quick close**: Show them time saved

### How to find them:
- Zillow: top agents in area
- Realtor.com: agents by city
- LinkedIn: "realtor" + location
- Instagram: #[city]realestate

### Outreach angle:
```
Hi {name},

Saw you're active in {area} real estate.

Quick question: how fast do you respond to new leads?

Our AI system responds in 60 seconds, 24/7. 
Agents using it close 30% more deals.

Want to see it in action?
```

### Pricing:
- Solo agent: $500/month
- Team: $1,500/month
- Brokerage: $3,000+/month
